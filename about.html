Our Story

Local Roots, Professional Excellence

Okanagan Construction was founded in 2005 by a group of local tradespeople who saw a need for a construction company that truly understood the unique challenges of building in the Okanagan Valley. From the hot, dry summers to the snowy winters, we knew that construction here required specific knowledge and techniques.

What started as a small framing crew has grown into a full-service construction company serving residential and commercial clients throughout Kelowna, West Kelowna, Peachland, Lake Country, and beyond. Despite our growth, we've maintained our commitment to personalized service and quality craftsmanship.

Today, we're proud to be one of Kelowna's most trusted construction companies, with a reputation built on honesty, reliability, and superior workmanship. Every project we undertake is a reflection of our commitment to excellence and our love for the Okanagan community.

Our Journey

Milestones

2005 - Company Founded

Started as a small framing crew with just 3 employees, focusing on residential framing in Kelowna.

2008 - Expanded Services

Added full general contracting services and completed our first custom home build from foundation to finish.

2012 - Commercial Division

Launched commercial construction division, completing our first major retail renovation in downtown Kelowna.

2018 - 300th Project

Celebrated completion of our 300th project. Team grew to 25 full-time employees and tradespeople.

2024 - Today

Serving the entire Okanagan Valley with a team of 35+, having completed over 500 projects and maintained 5-star reputation.
