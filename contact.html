Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical renovation take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A typical kitchen renovation takes 4-6 weeks, while a full home renovation may take 3-4 months. Custom home builds generally range from 8-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the quote process.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, applications, and inspections as part of our full-service approach. We navigate Kelowna and regional district building codes daily and ensure all work meets or exceeds local requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Okanagan Valley including Kelowna, West Kelowna, Peachland, Lake Country, Vernon, and Penticton. For larger commercial projects, we may travel further - contact us to discuss your location.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed with BC Housing, carry comprehensive general liability insurance, and maintain WorkSafe BC coverage. We can provide all documentation upon request for your peace of mind.
